About Me

A year ago, when I decided that I'd learn how to code, I didn't have a clear vision in mind for what exactly I wanted to do. At first, my interest was merely sparked because I found it satisfying to solve simple coding algorithms/challenges. But over time, as I immersed myself more into the subject, I became aware of the astounding possibilities that could be achieved through coding and software development; from creating simple web applications, to developing cutting edge technologies, anything seemed possible. To this day, I am excited by the sheer number of avenues available for me to challenge myself in this field.

In this time, I've continually been honing my skills. My current skillset includes (primarily) using Javascript, React/Redux, the MERN stack, Ruby on Rails, and SQL. Using the above technologies, I'm able to build full stack web applications from scratch. In the coming months and years, I am plan on continuing to learn new technologies, work on new projects, and further challenge myself.

As for my personal hobbies, I love playing online video games with my friends, I love to sing, and I love to eat good food!
